eSchool Extension for Chrome (for Arkansas K12 Schools)

Cognos DSN:

Data Source Name for navigating directly to Cognos.
This can be found in the address bar of your browser immediate after logging in to Cognos. Look for the section that says "dsn=".
Example:

https://cognos.domain.com/SPI/SPI_To_CRN.asp?dsn=YourDsnHere&appver=20.4
